Amara had always been fascinated by the rich, vibrant culture of Africa. As a young artist, she found herself drawn to the bold, abstract forms and mesmerizing patterns that permeated the continent's artistic traditions.

Africa

One day, while exploring a local museum, Amara stumbled upon an exhibition that would change the trajectory of her work. It was a Bauhaus-inspired showcase, where traditional African motifs had been reinterpreted through a modern, geometric lens.
